# Auto-Buy/Sell

## **AutoBuy**

AutoBuy enables instant token purchases using your predefined wallet, amount, and fee settings as soon as a new token is detected.

***

### **How it works**

Once enabled, AutoBuy will:

* Automatically execute a purchase when a contract address is sent or forwarded, including any link or message containing a contract address. Buybot messages are also supported.
* Bypass the confirmation step to ensure the transaction is processed immediately.
* Utilize your default wallet, pre-configured purchase amount, and selected priority fee settings.

***

## **Auto Sell — Take Profit & Stop Loss**

Auto Sell lets you automatically sell tokens when they reach your target price or fall to a stop-loss level. You can set multiple take-profit (TP) and stop-loss (SL) levels or create precise sell orders based on token amounts or portfolio percentages.

***

### **How It Works**

* **Take Profit (TP):** Sells automatically when the price reaches your target gain. Example: +50% sells when the price rises 50% above your entry.
* **Stop Loss (SL):** Sells automatically when the price falls to your set threshold. Example: -30% sells if the price drops 30%.

***

### **Setup**&#x20;

1. Go to **Settings**.

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (28) (3).png" alt="" width="395"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

2. Click on **Trading Settings**.

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (31) (1).png" alt="" width="402"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

3. Enable **Auto Sell** by toggling it on.

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (34).png" alt="" width="395"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

4. Configure your TP and SL rules, slippage, and Sell Protection as needed.

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (35).png" alt="" width="393"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

***

### Notes

* Only applies to new trades, not existing positions.
* Each purchase tracks its own TP/SL rules; multiple buys trigger separately.
* Clear or update previous presets after new buys to avoid double triggers.

Using Auto Sell together with Thor’s trailing stop-loss allows you to manage exits efficiently while keeping your funds active.
