# Auto-Sell

## **Auto Sell — Take Profit & Stop Loss**

Auto Sell lets you automatically sell tokens when they reach your target price or fall to a stop-loss level. You can set multiple take-profit (TP) and stop-loss (SL) levels or create precise sell orders based on token amounts or portfolio percentages.

***

### **How It Works**

* **Take Profit (TP):** Sells automatically when the price reaches your target gain. Example: +50% sells when the price rises 50% above your entry.
* **Stop Loss (SL):** Sells automatically when the price falls to your set threshold. Example: -30% sells if the price drops 30%.

***

### Notes

* Only applies to new trades, not existing positions.
* Each purchase tracks its own TP/SL rules; multiple buys trigger separately.
* Clear or update previous presets after new buys to avoid double triggers.

Using Auto Sell together with Thor’s trailing stop-loss allows you to manage exits efficiently while keeping your funds active.
